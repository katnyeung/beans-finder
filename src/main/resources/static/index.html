About This System

Coffee Finder aggregates and standardizes coffee product data from multiple roasters, providing a centralized database with SCA (Specialty Coffee Association) flavor wheel integration. Our knowledge graph enables complex queries like finding "honey-processed Geishas with pear-like sweetness."
